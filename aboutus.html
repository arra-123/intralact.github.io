The Legacy

Following years of deep-space exploration, the visionary research institute located ecipSe25—a breathtaking world that mirrors Earth’s natural beauty while offering extraordinary planetary wonders. We have secured exclusive rights to this world to establish eSpice25, a pinnacle of interstellar luxury designed for the galaxy’s elite. Our mission is to provide the backbone for this frontier, offering immersive experiences that challenge you to truly rethink reality and embrace the future.
